My Education

Currently, I attend NFA-PTECH at Newburgh Free Academy North where I will earn an Associate Degree of Applied Science (AAS) in Cybersecurity along with my high school diploma. To work towards my degree, I have been taking college courses through SUNY Orange since September 2017. Some college courses I have taken are ENG 101/102, MAT 101/102/121/122, CIT 100/105/112/116/118/203/217, CFR 221, CSS 223 and CRJ 101/111. As of January 2019, I currently have 31 college credits. While in PTECH, I have learned how to code in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Before I came to PTECH, I attended middle school at South Middle School where I was in honors.

I have also attended a leadership course called NYLT (National Youth Leadership Training) which is a week-long course that teaches you important skills needed to be a leader.

Over the summer of 2017, I took a Watson Analytics class. This class lasted the entire month of July. In this course, I learned how to use IBM Watson to analyze data. More specifically, I learned how to upload and download data, how to ask Watson questions, create graphs, charts, and tables, along with how to create displays. At the end of the course, I took and passed the IBM Watson test.

Also over the summer of 2017, I took an HTML/CSS/Web Design course from General Assembly. Through this course, I learned new coding techniques and strengthened my knowledge of how to code and design a functioning web page in HTML and CSS.
