About Me

Hello, my name is Kyle N. Villano. I am 15 years old and a sophomore in the Excelsior Academy at Newburgh Free Academy where I am learning how to code in HTML and CSS. I have also joined a programming club. When I am not in school, you can find me at my Boy Scout meetings, where I was the Senior Patrol Leader. Through this position I showed leadership, collaboration, and communication by planning and running meetings along with other events. I was elected by my fellow scouts in June 2016. My term ended in June 2017.

I am motivated to learn new things everyday. I always use curiosity to learn new things in all my classes. I also use analytical thinking to get my work done even when the going gets tough. Through this analytical thinking and curiosity, I have achieved many things. I was nominated and inducted into National Junior Honor Society in 6th grade. Also, one of my writing assignments, called “Letters to the President,” was featured in a radio segment with three of my classmates (link to radio segment). I regularly use self-management and responsibility to get not only my school work done, but also to complete tasks that people trust me to complete. Some of my long term goals are to become an Eagle scout by the time I am 16 years old (I am currently a Life Scout, the rank before Eagle) and get my degree in Applied Science in five years.
