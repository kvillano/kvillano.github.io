Boy Scouts

I started scouting back when I was in first grade. Scouting has taught me how to be independent in my work and given me a newfound appreciation for camping. One of my favorite campouts was in March 2016 when I went to Washington DC for five days. I enjoyed seeing our nation's capital and monuments.

In June 2016 I was elected to be the Senior Patrol Leader. This meant that I was the head scout for my troop. I had to plan and run the meetings every Monday along with appointing other people to leadership positions. In order to fulfill this position, I had to take a leadership course called NYLT in August 2016. NYLT stands for National Youth Leadership Training. In this week-long course, I learned how to be a better leader. Among the things that I learned were conflict resolution and a reflection tool called Start, Stop, Continue. My term as the Senior Patrol Leader ended in June 2017. I am currently the Junior Assistant Scoutmaster, a position only given to scouts who have demonstrated outstanding leadership skills. My main responsibility is to assist the Scoutmaster and Assistant Scoutmasters by providing supervision and support to the younger youth leaders.

In August 2018 I completed a major step to becoming an Eagle Scout, my Eagle Project. My project was an approximately Â¾ mile long nature trail at Chadwick Lake Park in Newburgh, New York. This trail is an extension of the main trail that reconnects back to the main trail. Chadwick Lake park determined the path of the trail and it was my job to clear the trail, install a 6-foot footbridge, install trail markers and signs, and name the trail. Through this project, I learned important life skills like how to manage money as I was responsible for acquiring donations and raising funds. While working on this project I used professional skills like leadership, to lead my volunteers, and responsibility, to complete my project to the best of my ability. If you would like to see before and after pictures, as well as in progress photos, click here.

I finally achieved my long term goal of becoming an Eagle Scout on October 10th, 2018 after many years of determination and commitment.
